About us

Lithion Power is India's largest "Battery as a service" operator, providing lithium ion batteries for e-bikes and 3 wheelers. We provide power to Electric Vehicles by battery swap.

We have a network of Lithion Swapping Points (LSP) across North India. Our customers drive to a LSP and walk away with a charged battery in less than 5 minutes. Lithion is working with large operators and OEMs in making an EV only India -- a reality by 2030.
